About Monkeys

Like most species there are many types of monkeys in the world but they all live in different environments. The monkeys we will be referring to are the ones that live in the jungle. The monkeys that live in the jungle have a diet of plants, fruits, insects,bird eggs, nuts, and flowers. Those are their main source of food in the jungle. There are many types of monkeys in the jungles but the main ones are Spider, Howler, Squirrel monkeys.

Fun Facts About Monkeys

There are more than 260 different types of monkeys. Monkeys can be divided into two groups, Old World monkeys that live in Africa and Asia, and New World monkeys that live in South America. Some monkeys actually live on the ground and not in the trees like others.
