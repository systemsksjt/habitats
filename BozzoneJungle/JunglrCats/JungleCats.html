About Jungle Cats

Not all Jungle cats solo habitat is the Jungle some live in the desert and can survive in different habitats much like different animals. They do prefer swampy environment much like what the jungle provides. Jungle Cats include tigers, leopards, lions, cheetah, jaguar. There are more cats that live in the jungle but those are the main ones and as we know they do live in different places as well but the most unfortunate is with most large cat species there are more in human captivity than there are in the wild. This is a problem because a lot of these cats are not treated properly and they become over breed.

Fun Facts About Jungle Cats

Jungle cats are helpful humans because their main source of food is rodents, controlling pest populations. Jungle cats have claws on both their fore-and hind-legs that make it easy for them to climb trees both upward and downward. Jungle cats also like to sit in the sun in the winter and diving in the water to catch fish.
